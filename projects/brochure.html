Korn und Schrot

  • Back to all projects

For the final exam of my apprenticeship, I was assigned to design a 12-page brochure for the German corn mill "Korn und Schrot" (english: corn and grist).

I was given the format, the text and a selection of pictures which I was allowed to use however I wanted. The brochure featured informational texts about the corn mill as well as a section with some recipes for the user to bake.

The first step was to create sketches and ideas of different layouts and designs. After diverging, it was time to converge and decide on one idea to pursue.

I combined my knowledge about design elements like typography, usage of images, usage of colour and working with programmes like Adobe InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop to create my final design. Apart from the visual design of the brochure, it was important to know about printing techniques, colour management, paper types, folding types and bleed, amongst others to create a high-quality printing product.
